Agile methods help teams manage work so that they can respond to the needs of citizens. It's an alternative to managing projects in long phases, called waterfall project management.

Sprint planning helps teams understand their capacity and what user stories they can work on in the sprint. Each sprint typically lasts 1 to 2 weeks.

Retrospectives give the team an opportunity to reflect on how they worked and identify improvements. They're usually held at the end of each sprint.
